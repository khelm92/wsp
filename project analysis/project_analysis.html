Designer: Kasey Helm

Client: Signs by Design

Document: Project Analysis

Site Intent

  • Client goals: The client wishes to present their content in a way to make it appealing to users and coax them to use their design services.
  • User goals: The user wants to see examples of work Signs by Design has done, be able to get a quote for work they need done, and find contact information and directions for the business.
  • My redesign: I plan to streamline the whole website and give it a graphical overhaul. Currently, the website has several pages and links just for picture galleries; I plan to change that to just one page. Same thing with their services. Also, the navigation is just thrown in the middle of the page so I want to completely redo that.
